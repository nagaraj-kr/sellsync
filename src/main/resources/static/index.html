Our Business Partners

Ambuja Cement is one of India's leading cement manufacturers, known for producing high-quality cement for construction. It was founded in 1983 and later became a part of the Holcim Group (now Adani Group). Ambuja Cement is recognized for its sustainability initiatives, eco-friendly products, and advanced technology in cement production.
